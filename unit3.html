Intro to App Design

This unit is an introduction to programming and app design with a heavy focus on important skills like debugging, pair programming, and user testing. Learn how to design user interfaces and write event-driven programs in App Lab and then design a project that teaches your classmates about a topic of your choosing. students will dive into the world of programming and app design, honing essential skills like debugging, collaboration, and user testing. Through App Lab, they will learn to craft engaging user interfaces and write event-driven programs that bring their ideas to life. Building on these foundational skills, students will design and develop their own project, choosing a topic that they are passionate about and want to share with their classmates. This hands-on approach allows students to take ownership of their learning and develop a sense of pride and accomplishment as they see their projects come to fruition.
